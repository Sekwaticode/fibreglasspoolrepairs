Welcome to Fibre Glass Pool Repairs

Expert Fibreglass Pool Restoration and Refurbishment in Gauteng

Fibreglass Pool Repairs is a trusted expert in restoring and refurbishing pools throughout Gauteng. Our team specializes in bringing worn or damaged pools back to their original condition, using high-quality materials and techniques to ensure long-lasting durability and customer satisfaction.

We are committed to providing personalized solutions that cater to your pool’s unique needs. Whether it’s minor repairs or complete refurbishments, we handle each project with meticulous attention to detail, transforming your pool into a beautiful, functional space for you to enjoy year-round.

Our Services

We Provide The Following Services

Revitalize your pool's appearance with our specialized pool coating services. We apply high-quality, UV-resistant paint to restore color and protect the fiberglass surface, giving your pool a fresh, vibrant look that lasts.

Pool Coating

Eliminate tough stains and discolorations with our fibreglass pool lining service. We use safe, effective bleaching agents to restore your pool's original brightness and cleanliness, enhancing its appearance and ensuring a healthier swimming environment.

Fibre Glass Pool Lining

Our comprehensive pool cleaning service ensures a pristine, safe swimming environment. We remove debris, scrub surfaces, and balance water chemistry, maintaining your pool's beauty and preventing costly repairs caused by neglect.

Pool Cleaning

Ensure your pool's circulation system functions efficiently with our pool pump services. We provide installation, repair, and maintenance, using energy-efficient, high-quality pumps tailored to your pool’s specific needs, ensuring clean and safe water.

Pool Pump

Protect your pool with our durable pool covers. Designed to fit perfectly, our covers prevent debris, maintain water temperature, and reduce evaporation, extending your pool’s lifespan while ensuring safety and convenience.

Pool Covering

Enhance your pool’s beauty with our expert mosaic tile services. We specialize in custom designs, precise installation, and durable finishes, adding a luxurious touch to any pool
